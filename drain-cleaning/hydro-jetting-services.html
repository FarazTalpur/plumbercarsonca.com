Why Choose Hydro Jetting?

Hydro jetting is a powerful and efficient method for clearing drains and pipes. Using high-pressure water streams, this service ensures the removal of grease, sludge, roots, and debris. Here's why you should consider hydro jetting:

  • Highly Effective: Unlike traditional methods, hydro jetting thoroughly cleans pipes, preventing future blockages.
  • Environmentally Friendly: This process uses only water, negating the need for harsh chemicals.
  • Cost-Effective: By preventing future plumbing issues, you save money on repeated services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Hydro jetting is a method used to clean and clear clogged or slow sewage lines using high-pressure water streams.

Unlike snaking, which only breaks up clogs, hydro jetting thoroughly cleans pipes by removing grease, scale, and other debris, providing a more comprehensive cleaning.

Hydro jetting is generally safe for most types of pipes, including PVC, steel, and cast iron, if the pipes are in good condition. However, it might not be suitable for older or fragile pipes.

Regular hydro jetting is recommended every one to two years for maintenance, but your plumber can provide a more specific schedule depending on your unique circumstances.

When performed by a professional, hydro jetting is safe and unlikely to damage your plumbing system. It is important to have an experienced technician assess your pipes before proceeding.
